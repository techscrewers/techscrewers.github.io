About Us

We Are a Technical Startup.

Modern problems require modern solution!!! We all are living in 21st century and in our daily life we come across various problems of which we are not able to find out some exceptional solution. Hence to come up with the best possible solution of the problem our company Tech Screwers is here. Its an initiative by two undergraduate students along with a team of handful but quality people who have a good command over various technologies like app development, web development, data science, data analysts, graphic designers etc. We will be using our technical skills to come up with the best possible solution to your problem. So any company or an individual can contact us for the best solution to there problem. We basically aim to solve all the real life problems faced by the common people in their day to day life.
